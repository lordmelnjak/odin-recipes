Simple Hamburger

Description

Simple hamburger ideal for sunday NFL games or hanging out with your family. Meal is finished under in 30 minutes.

All ingredients are very easy to find. Additionally you can add a portion of french fries to the meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 kg of ground beef
  • 8 large hamburger buns
  • 2 spoons of sunflower oil
  • Tabasco hot sauce
  • Sour cream sauce
  • Salt, pepper
  • Jalapeno peppers
  • White onion
  • Cucumber
  • Any other sauce as per your wish

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill for high heat. When the grill is hot, roast the meat.
  2. Mix all the vegetables.
  3. Put the meat in the hamburger buns and then put vegetables and the sauces on top of it.
  4. Repeat that 7x more times.
  5. As per your wish, add french fries as a side meal.
